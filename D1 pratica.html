Focaccia with Caramelised Onions,
Cranberrries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelised onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Images: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recies That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a pairing knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all
    in the dough ingridients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the
    dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate
    the last of the flour...
